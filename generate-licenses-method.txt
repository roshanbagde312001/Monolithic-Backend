Generate a signed license:
JAVA_HOME=/Library/Java/JavaVirtualMachines/jdk-17.jdk/Contents/Home \
java -cp target/classes com.appdefend.backend.license.OfflineLicenseGenerator \
./private-key.pem AIRGAP-DEFAULT "Coding vedha" 250 2026-04-06


JAVA_HOME=/Library/Java/JavaVirtualMachines/jdk-17.jdk/Contents/Home \
mvn -q -Dmaven.repo.local=.m2 -DskipTests compile exec:java \
-Dexec.mainClass=com.appdefend.backend.license.OfflineLicenseGenerator \
-Dexec.args="./private-key.pem AIRGAP-DEFAULT \"Coding vedha\" 250 2026-04-06"

Arguments are:

privateKeyPemPath
deploymentId
customerName
maxNamedUsers
validUntil in YYYY-MM-DD
